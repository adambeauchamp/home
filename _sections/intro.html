Hi! I'm Adam Beauchamp,
a librarian, historian, and denizen of the Gulf South.

This website is a place to highlight some of my projects and is itself a work in progress.
I'm currently learning to use GitHub, R, and other digital humanitites tools and techniques to expand my practice as a librarian and my scholarship as a historian.

The map you see here is the Carte générale du territoire d'Orléans comprenant aussi la Floride Occidentale et une portion du territoire du Mississippi by Barthélémy Lafon in 1806. This copy is held at the Library of Congress, Geography and Map Division, and can be viewed or downloaded from the Library of Congress website.
